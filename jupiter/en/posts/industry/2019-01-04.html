Future Growth for Global Cloud TV Market

April 2019 - According to a report by Mordor Intelligence, the global cloud TV market is expected to grow 9,12% in five years (2018-2023), this includes Public cloud, Private Cloud, and Hybrid Cloud in sections such as Telecom, Entertainment and Media, Information Technology, and Consumer Television. The increasing number of smart devices and the easy installment are among the biggest contributors of the growth of cloud TV market. The versatility of cloud platforms means it could be easily equipped on any smart device, and the integration of the cloud TV services will enable the consumers to access every single aspect of internet and television through a single device.

The Mordor Intelligence report also shows that the entertainment and media are expected to be one of the primary areas for applications of cloud TV. The high definition streaming and lack of delay in the broadcasting services of cloud TV provides huge competitive advantages over the conventional set top boxes.
