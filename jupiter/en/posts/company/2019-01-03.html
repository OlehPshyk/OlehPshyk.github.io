Vienna, Austria

In March 2019 Jupiter Media GmbH visited Cable Tech trade fair in Spielberg, Steiermark. This year's Cable Tech was hosted by the Austrian local company AICALL and sponsored by local TV stations such as ATV. Over 30 companies participated in this 2-day event. Jupiter Media exchanged knowledge and experience with exhibiting companies in this professional and friendly environment and established business contacts with clients and companies, with which we hope to develop future cooperation. We were very happy to participate in this trade fair, as it provided a good networking opportunity for newly founded companies like Jupiter Media. We are looking forward to next year's Cable Tech.
