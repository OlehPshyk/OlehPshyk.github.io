How It Works

you share

You choose how to donate your anonymous Amazon shopping history.

we pool

We pool your anonymous shopping history with others' to reveal shopping trends, which brands pay for.

kids win

The proceeds of your data fund a donation to NUDM's 2018 partners, Cradles to Crayons and ECF, equal to 1% of your Amazon purchases!

Your data is kept 100% anonymous and secure

Your personal information (like name and address) is automatically removed from your shopping data. This means you won't receive any additional advertisements, spam, or messages.

Any personal information you provide is encrypted using industry-wide best practices, and this data is never shared with any brands or retailers.

NORTHWESTERN UNIVERSITY
DANCE MARATHON

Now in its 44th year, NUDM is one of the largest student-run philanthropies in the nation. NUDM’s mission is to unite the Northwestern, Evanston, and the Greater Chicagoland Communities in philanthropic fundraising, service, awareness, and education.

Every year, 1000+ dancers and committee members come together to dance for 30 hours straight to benefit NUDM’s primary rotating and secondary beneficiaries. This year, NUDM is excited to support:

Cradles to Crayons, NUDM's primary beneficiary, is a nonprofit that provides children living in low-income situations with the essential items they need to survive, such as clothes, toy, shoes, and school supplies.

The Evanston Community Foundation is NUDM's long-standing secondary beneficiary. The ECF is dedicated to helping Evanston thrive as a vibrant, inclusive and just community.

About Data Does Good

Our mission is to create new sources of funding for charitable causes by helping people see their data as a valuable resource that can be used for the public good.

We're proud to be a Public Benefit Corporation, which means we're a hybrid between a nonprofit and a for-profit organization.

Scott, Dylan, and Chris formed the Data Does Good team at Stanford University.

Collectively, their experience includes market research, software engineering, consumer advocacy, lightning strike survival, and pig farming.

Data Does Good was founded on the belief that people should be able to do something positive with their data.

Across millions of websites, people generate an incredible amount of valuable information but don't have a good way to do anything with it.

We also believe that when pooled together, our data can positively affect the world. When individuals choose to share information, we can collectively shape a brighter future.

So we developed a transparent system that allows people to donate the cash value of their data to important causes in an anonymous and secure way. It's a small step toward doing something with your data that can have a big impact.

Our three promises to you are:

  • Privacy: We protect your privacy and take security seriously.
  • Transparency: We never collect data without your knowledge.
  • Fairness: We will always give back a majority of our revenue.
