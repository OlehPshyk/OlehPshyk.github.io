Publisher Service

Vice Versa is one of the largest and oldest distributors fully dedicated to art publications and artist’s books. Based in Berlin, Vice Versa specializes in architecture, art, design, fashion, film, photography and theory. We carry a carefully curated list of publishers, who share our passion for printed matter. We sell books and magazines to consumers, bookshops, museums, libraries, and companies in Germany, Austria, and Switzerland and fulfill orders worldwide. If you have any inquieries please email us, we are looking forward to hear from you.
